# CE Training Workshops — Design Resources


## Company name and blurb


**CE Training Workshops** is a continuing-education platform for social workers, mental-health professionals, and other helping professionals. It delivers live webinars, self-study courses, and subscription-based learning through a WordPress, WooCommerce, and Sensei LMS experience. The product should feel credible, supportive, accessible, and human—professional enough for regulated continuing education without feeling clinical or institutional.


Website: https://cetrainingworkshops.com/

### Typography


- Primary: Open Sans
- Fallback: Arial, sans-serif
- CTA text: Arial, bold


### Visual direction


- Clean, welcoming, and trustworthy
- High legibility and accessible contrast
- Rounded controls and restrained shadows
- Dark Azure for structure and primary UI
- Apple Green and Azure for supportive accents and progress states
- Burnt Orange for calls to action
- Mist Green and Mist Gray for soft panels and backgrounds
- Avoid overly playful, corporate, or hospital-like styling
